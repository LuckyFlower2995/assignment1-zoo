Penguin

(The image above displays the emperor penguin, the tallest and heaviest species of penguin.)

The penguin is an aquatic, flightless bird who exclusively resides in the Southern Hemisphere. It can adapt in both land and sea and feeds on small aquatic life-forms, such as fish, krill, and squid. Penguins hunt either in groups or by itself, and during breeding season, penguins nest in huge groups and establish nesting territory amongst each other.
